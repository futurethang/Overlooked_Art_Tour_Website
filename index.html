About the Tour

Explore Seattle’s historic Pioneer Square while uncovering the stories behind our unique hatch-covers, overlooked art, and underground portals.

Gallery

See and Learn

Century old Designs

Designs of pure utility are easy to overlook. We want to share our love of the aesthetic quality and craftsmanship of forgotten foundries.

Commissioned Art

Since the 1970s, Seattle has commissioned talented artists to breathe life into the streets. We highlight some of the most beautiful examples and teach more about this amazing program!

Wide Variety

Seattle has dozens of hatchcover designs, from the contemporary and brand new to the antique and worn. We take you through one of most dense and diverse areas to get an insider’s view.

Local History

Though now heavily produced overseas, local iron works were once an integral part of Seattle's 19th century growth. We'll see antique iron that has beautified the streets for generations.
